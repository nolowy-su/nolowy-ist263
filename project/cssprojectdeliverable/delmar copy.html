San Diego Surf Guide

Information about Del Mar

Del Mar Beach is located right next to the famous Torrey Pines State Park. The gorgeous white sand beach is one of the most popular in San Diego as it's downtown area filled with restaurants. The beach is right along the historic Highway 101, also known as the coast highway. The beach is lined with gorgeous beach houses on the northern half, while the southern half is overlooked by cliffs. The Amtrak Coaster train can be seen running along these cliffs and through downtown Del Mar. The Beach is a popular one for surfers. It is a more welcoming surf break for beginner surfers and on the right day it can create perfect beginner conditions.

Things to do at Del Mar

  • Take a walk along the train tracks, overlooking the cliffs.
  • Dine at restaurants in the downtown area.
  • Visit the nearby Del Mar Thoroughbred Club, where horse races are held.
  • Visit the nearby dog friendly beach with your four legged friends!
