Blacks Beach Guide

Information about Blacks Beach

Blacks Beach is one of San Diego's hidden gems. The beach is also one of the hardest to get to. Located north of La Jolla Shores, there is no main entrance, and no parking lot nearby. The entrance is located off of La Jolla Farms road, an affluent neighborhood where celebrities like Alicia Keys own properties. Beach goers can park on the street and walk to the entrance. The entrance leads you to a half mile, downhill walk to the beach. Be aware, Blacks beach is a nude beach, so it might not be a family beach. However, it is known as one of the best surf breaks in the nation. An underwater canyon offshore channels waves into perfectly formed sections when the right swell comes through. Surfers flock to the beach when a good swell comes in. The million dollar mansions lofted on the massive cliffs overlooking the beach, combined with clear water, good surf, and frequent dolphin sightings make this one of the most epic surf spots in the country.

Things to do at Blacks Beach

  • The most popular thing to do around blacks beach is to surf and enjoy the incredible waves. Be careful not to get in the way of any locals, they tend to be territorial over this sacred spot.
  • Go on a hike! There's some interesting trails around the area, but be careful the can be steep and unkept.
  • Lay out on the beach and enjoy the scenery. The majestic cliffs overhanging the beach offer a beautiful backdrop to a beach day!
  • Go see the hang gliders at the nearby Torrey Pines Gliderport
  • Play a round of golf at the famous Torrey Pines Golf Course
